# Five-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00 - Hook

AI is lowering the cost of producing papers. Paper Hunter uses AI-era tooling in the opposite direction: evidence-first research-integrity review.

## 0:45 - Upload

Open the workbench and launch the synthetic sample. Mention that a real public case can be reviewed locally without redistributing copyrighted PDFs.

## 1:30 - Pipeline

Show the task moving through PDF parsing, image extraction, image forensics, citation checks, and report generation.

## 2:15 - Evidence

Open the evidence center. Show a suspicious image-similarity card with method, confidence, location, assets, and recommended action.

## 3:15 - Review

Open image review. Show side-by-side comparison and generated difference asset.

## 4:00 - Report

Open the report tab. Point out cautious wording, method notes, and the disclaimer. Use browser print/save-as-PDF for export.

## 4:45 - Close

Paper Hunter does not let AI judge people. It lets AI help reviewers find, explain, and reproduce evidence.
